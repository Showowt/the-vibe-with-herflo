Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

The Vibe Check Dating Show with HerFlo ("we," "us," or "our"), produced in partnership with HOT 702.5 FM Risky Radio, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you apply to participate in our show.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you submit an application, we collect:

  • Full name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Age and date of birth
  • Gender identity
  • Relationship preferences (who you're seeking)
  • Location (country and state/region)
  • Responses to application questions (energy description, why you want to participate)

Payment Information

Application fees are processed through Cash App. We do not store your payment card details. Payment confirmation is tracked separately.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process and evaluate your show application
  • Contact you regarding your application status
  • Coordinate logistics if selected for filming
  • Match you with compatible participants based on our energy-based methodology
  • Communicate about future seasons or related opportunities
  • Improve our application process and show format
Important: If selected, portions of your personal story and information may be shared during the show broadcast on radio, YouTube, and social media platforms. You will sign additional release forms before filming.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Production Team: Staff involved in casting and show production
  • HOT 702.5 FM: Our radio station partner for broadcast purposes
  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist with database hosting (Supabase), email communications, and technical operations
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews

5. Data Retention

We retain your application data for:

  • Selected Participants: Duration of show involvement plus 3 years
  • Non-Selected Applicants: Up to 2 years (for future season consideration)
  • Withdrawn Applications: 90 days after withdrawal request

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw your application at any time
  • Opt out of future communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@vibecheck.show or herflo@hot702.5fm.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal tracking:

  • Essential cookies for form functionality
  • Analytics to understand site usage (anonymized)

We do not use advertising trackers or sell tracking data.

8. Age Requirement

You must be at least 25 years old to apply for The Vibe Check. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 25. If we discover such data was collected, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.
